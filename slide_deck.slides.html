Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at the characteristics of video games that could be used to increae their sales. The main focus was on the three Cs of video games: Platform,Genre,and Publisher.

Dataset Overview¶

The data consisted of sales and attributes of approximately 16,000 round-cut video games. The attributes included the three Cs of video games, as well as additional attributes such as Na sales, Na sales, and Na sales. Thirty-nine data points were removed from the analysis due to inconsistencies or missing information.

(increase sales around years)¶

We find that the video games industry is on the increase, and there are many companies entering that industry, and with the passage of years we notice an increase in the number of games produced, which leads to an increase in the purchase because it has become a profitable industry,and this is evident on the graph, which shows that sales are increasing every year
